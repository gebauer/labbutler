# Getting started

## Signing in

You sign in with your **email address and password**. Accounts are created by a lab
manager (see [adding members](lab-administration.md#members)); when you are added,
LabButler emails you a welcome message with a **set-password link**. If the link has
expired, or you ever forget your password, use **Forgot password?** on the sign-in
page to email yourself a fresh reset link.

!!! note "Locked out?"
    After several failed sign-in attempts (5 by default) your IP + username
    combination is locked for a cool-off period (1 hour by default) to block
    password-guessing. Wait it out, or ask the server administrator to lift the
    lock early.

## The dashboard

After signing in you land on a **role-aware dashboard**: it only shows widgets that
are relevant to what you can do in the lab, newest entries first. Depending on your
permissions you may see, for example:

- **My pending requests** — things you have asked for that are still awaiting
  approval or handling, so you can chase them.
- **My requests in progress** — your orders on their way through
  ordering and delivery.
- **Requests to approve** — for approvers: requests that need a decision.
- **Requests to order** — for purchase coordinators: approved requests waiting to
  be placed, including ones forwarded to you.
- **Expecting deliveries** — ordered goods that should arrive soon, ready to check
  in.

Each widget links straight to the relevant filtered list, and the most common actions
(raise a request, search inventory) are one click from here.

## Switching labs

If you are a member of more than one lab, the navigation bar shows a **lab
switcher**. Everything you see — inventory, requests, settings — always belongs to
the currently selected lab.

## Your account settings

Under **Account settings** (your name in the navigation bar) you can:

- Set a **display name** — how you appear to colleagues in lists, comments, and
  emails, instead of your raw email address.
- Manage **email notification preferences** per lab — which procurement emails you
  receive. Only the categories you can actually act on are offered (approvers see
  approval notifications, requesters see status updates). See
  [Notifications](notifications.md).

## Signing out

**Sign out** from the account menu returns you to the start page.
