# Welcome — your first ten minutes

New to LabButler? This page is the short version of everything you need on day
one. LabButler shows it to you **in the app the first time you sign in** (and it
stays reachable there under `/welcome/`); the chapters linked below hold the
details when you need them.

## 1 · Introduce yourself

Set a **display name** under Account settings (your name in the navigation bar),
so colleagues see your name instead of your raw email address in lists, comments,
and emails. See [Getting started](getting-started.md#your-account-settings).

## 2 · Find things

The **Inventory** holds every item in the lab. Search by name, CAS number, or the
item ID printed on the container label — that ID is
[frozen forever](index.md#frozen-item-ids), so even a label from years ago always
finds its record. Details in [Inventory](inventory.md).

## 3 · Order things

Need something the lab doesn't have? Raise a **procurement request**. It moves
through approval, ordering, and delivery; your dashboard shows where it stands,
and checking in the delivery puts the item straight into the inventory. The full
workflow is in [Procurement requests](procurement.md).

## 4 · Know what you can do

Everything you see is governed by the **roles** your lab manager gave you. If a
button described in this guide is missing, check **Account settings → Effective
permissions** to see which rights you hold, then ask your lab manager. The role
system is explained in the [overview](index.md#roles-and-permissions).
