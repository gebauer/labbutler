{# Feature grid #}

Inventory that fits your lab

Track chemicals, consumables, and equipment with locations, tags, custom fields, and GHS hazard statements.

Procurement without the paper trail

Request, approve, and order in one workflow — with budgets, vendors, and email notifications at every step.

Painless migration

Import existing data from LabSuit or any spreadsheet with a guided column mapper and a dry-run preview before anything is saved.

Built for shared labs

Multiple labs on one instance, each with its own members, roles, and fine-grained permissions.

A complete audit trail

Every change is recorded in an append-only log, so you can always answer who changed what, and when.

Your server, your data

Runs entirely on your own infrastructure. No third-party cloud, no per-seat fees, no data leaving the building.
